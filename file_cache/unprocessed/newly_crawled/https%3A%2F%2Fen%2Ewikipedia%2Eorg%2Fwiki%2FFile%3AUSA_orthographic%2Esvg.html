DescriptionUSA orthographic.svg
English: A map of the hemisphere centred on -100, 45, using an orthographic projection, created using gringer's Perl script with Natural Earth Data (1:50000 resolution, simplified to 0.25px). The United States (the contiguous United States, Alaska, and Hawaii; not including Puerto Rico and other territories) is highlighted in green.
