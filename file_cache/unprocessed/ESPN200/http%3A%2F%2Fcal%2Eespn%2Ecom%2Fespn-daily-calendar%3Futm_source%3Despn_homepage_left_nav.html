ESPN Daily Calendar

Add to your Outlook Calendar

After clicking Outlook

  1. Outlook will automatically open
  2. In the popup, click Yes
  3. The new calendar will be added to the panel on the left

Having trouble?

  1. Open Outlook Calendar
  2. Click Open Calendar and select From Internet
  3. Click New Calendar Subscription
  4. Copy and Paste the link below, then click Subscribe

Outlook for Mac does not support dynamic calendars. We recommend texting the calendar to your mobile device.

Add to your Apple Calendar

After clicking Apple

  1. Apple Calendar will automatically open
  2. On the pop-up that appears click Subscribe
  3. Another pop-up will appear, click Ok (here you can change the title, colour, and alert settings if you would like)
  4. Your new calendar will added to the list of calendars on the left hand side

Having trouble?

  1. Open Apple Calendar
  2. Click File
  3. Click New Calendar Subscription
  4. Copy and Paste the link below, then click Subscribe
