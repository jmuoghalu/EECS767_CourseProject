navigation , search     this is a list of seasons completed by the buffalo bills  american football franchise . the list documents the season-by-season records of the bills' franchise from 1960 to present, including postseason records, and league awards for individual players or head coaches. the bills finished their most recent season ( 2015 ) with a record of eight wins and eight losses.
